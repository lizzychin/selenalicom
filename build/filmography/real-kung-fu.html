Synopsis

Wing Chun Master Wong Wah Po (Yuen Wah)'s successor Leung Tsan (Yuen Biao) became involved in a murder case and therefore fleed to Fushan, to seek refuge with his master's senior Leung Yee Tai (Bryan Leung). In order to conceal his identity to investigate the truth, Tsan worked as a cook in merchant Chan's family, where he sees his lover Cheung Kin Hei (Maggie Siu). After Tsan's reunion with Hei, he's shocked to find out that she already got married. While Hei became jealous when a young beautiful princess Fu Chat Ho Yuet (Selena Lee) appeared besides Tsan. turns out that Hei thought that Tsan fleed their marriage that day and followed him till Fushan, and in order to save herself from villians she ends up having to agree a fake marriage with Mr. Chan. After their marriage, Mr. Chan died and Hei became a widow, she wholeheartedly helped reform his delinquent son Chan Wah Shun (Timmy Hung). After Tsan's investigations, the truth of the case comes to the light. However, he finds out that the murderer has killed Tai. Tsan was in an inexplicable grief, he swore he will take revenge for this Wing Chun master.
